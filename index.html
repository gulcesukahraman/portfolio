ABOUT

I graduated from the Department of Industrial Design at Yeditepe University. Through internships at Baykar Technology and GEMAK Group, I gained experience in product development, manufacturing processes, and production-oriented design.

Following this, I worked as a creative director in the textile industry, acquiring knowledge in website design and e-commerce. Digital design and branding projects further strengthened my multifaceted perspective.

With my creative and analytical approach, I aim to develop innovative products that combine technology, functionality, and aesthetics, and to create value within a dynamic team.
